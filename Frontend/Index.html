WHY SUKALYA?

In rural India, healthcare challenges remain severe. Over 68% of the population lives in villages, yet many lack access to reliable health guidance. People often struggle due to:

  • Language barriers ‚Äì Most resources are available only in English or Hindi, leaving many local dialect speakers excluded.
  • Poor connectivity ‚Äì Rural areas suffer from weak internet access, making online apps unreliable.
  • Overloaded helplines ‚Äì Government medical helplines face heavy call volumes, leading to delays.
  • Low awareness ‚Äì Preventive healthcare practices and early disease detection are often neglected.

SUKALYA addresses these problems head-on by providing an AI-powered health assistant that works in local languages, via SMS/WhatsApp, with voice/text support, and is available 24/7.

VISION AND MISSION

Vision

To make healthcare guidance as accessible and simple as sending a text message, empowering rural communities with reliable, real-time information.

Mission

  • Delivering multilingual, easy-to-understand health advice.
  • Enabling early disease detection through awareness.
  • Providing real-time outbreak alerts for community safety.
  • Reducing the pressure on overburdened medical staff and helplines.
